Current vaccination records for your pet(s) are not required, but is helpful to ensure the pet receives the correct vaccinations. Pet Licensing

Cats and other pets can be voluntarily licensed through our program and must be renewed each year. A license not only helps get your lost pet home, it also supports Foothills Animal Shelter so we can continue our life-saving care to thousands of homeless animals. This program is brought to you in partnership with your local animal control and management agencies.
